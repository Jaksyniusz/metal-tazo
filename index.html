About Me and Us

I am, as a humble member of Metal Tazo bordello-metal band, pleased to welcome You on the first official web page.

Metal Tazo was founded in 2004 after a short quarrel beetwen Fakson and me. He got so angry that convinced my school desk class mate Danio and his school desk mate Tyxen to record a song with special dedication for me. So that how it began, after less than a month we gave two concerts for our class. We weren't a typical musicians, because we played without ordinary instruments. That made our songs in some weird way different than other, maybe due to its rudimentary tons.

My name is Konrad and I decided to create my first portfolio as a page of my old band, not only to pay a tribute to MT, but I desired it from the very first day I started coding. Hope that one day, after months of practise I will make its look far more professional.
